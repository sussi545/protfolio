More Than Two Brain

Octopus

Octopus has three hearts. Octopus has nine brains. consists of one main brain to analyze and make decisions while the other brain process every information obtained from each arm. Two-thirds of the octopus's brain cells are in the arm..

leech's

leech's internal body is divided into 32 segments, and in each segment their brains has connected to each other. So anatomically, a leech has a brain that has 32 parts. But because each part can regulate its respective segments, then in physiology you could say that leeches have 32 brains!

Mormyrinae

The Peters' elephantnose fish, Gnathonemus petersii, has the largest brain-to-body weight ratio of all known vertebrates. Mormyrinae is the largest subfamily in the Osteoglossiformes order with around 170 species.
